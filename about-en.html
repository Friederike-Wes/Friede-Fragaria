About

I combine field botany with illustration, using fineliner and watercolor. On my postcards I experiment with natural pigments from beetroot, turmeric, red cabbage, spinach, and other plants. I also play with pH-based color shifts, so every card is unique.

My background is scientific: I studied landscape ecology in Muenster and completed my master's in botany in Innsbruck. I love details and scientifically accurate depictions of nature.
