Workshop at Cafe Spiral

We illustrate plants precisely with fineliner and then color them with handmade pigments from beetroot, turmeric, red cabbage, spinach, and other plant materials. We also experiment with pH-based color shifts and drawing styles in the wonderful atmosphere of Cafe Spiral in Innsbruck. The workshop fee is 40 Euro, including materials.
